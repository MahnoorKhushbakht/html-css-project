LUCKY SHRUB

Based in Tuscon, Arizona, Lucky Shrub is a medium-sized garden design firm that specializes in garden design and creation, maintenance and landscaping. The company also runs a small plant nursery that sells indoor and outdoor plants, making them a one-stop shop for clients to "transform any space into an oasis you can be proud of". Lucky Shrub was started by a husband and wife team, Jason and Maria, who share a long-time love for plants. Jason is the garden architect. He creates and oversees all designs while managing his team of landscapers. Maria manages all the marketing for the company and oversees the nursery.

Vegetable Garden

A vegetable garden is designed for growing edible plants, such as vegetables, herbs, and fruits. These gardens are usually focused on providing fresh produce for consumption and can range from small plots in backyards to larger-scale gardens for sustainable food production.

Flower Garden

Flower gardens are created for the purpose of growing ornamental flowers and plants, primarily for their beauty and visual appeal. They can feature a wide variety of flowering plants, including annuals, perennials, shrubs, and bulbs, arranged in aesthetically pleasing patterns and color schemes.

Herb Garden

Herb gardens are specifically dedicated to growing various herbs that are commonly used for culinary, medicinal, or aromatic purposes. They often include herbs like basil, mint, rosemary, thyme, and parsley, and can be incorporated into larger gardens or designed as standalone herb beds.
